01 — About

Tufan Tuğra İskender

I am a game developer, gamer, and founder of iBright Games, with a proven track record of guiding projects from concept to release and supporting live updates.

What defines my approach is simple: I enjoy creating the kinds of games I already love playing. This dual perspective — both as a developer and as a gamer — allows me to design systems that are technically robust while remaining deeply engaging for players. Whether it’s building branching dialogue frameworks, refining combat mechanics, or streamlining production pipelines, I focus on turning creative ideas into experiences that resonate with the communities I’m part of.

I thrive in collaborative environments where storytelling and engineering meet, and I bring both technical expertise and player insight to every project.

I also go by Sasete, a name that reflects my identity in gaming communities and creative work.
